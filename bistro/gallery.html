Not is our food good, it's also good-looking! Our patrons often stop to admire our fare with a quick Instagram before digging in. We've collected a few of our favorite shots here.

Our Baked Goods


We start our day at the crack of dawn to bake our own muffins, bread, and dinner rolls. Loaves not used that day are donated to the local food shelter.

Our Burgers


People come from all over to enjoy our mouth-watering burgers. We grind our own locally-sourced organic beef and turkey so you know it's fresh and free from fillers and other nonsense. Go for one of our creative topping combos or stick with the classics.

Catch of the Day


Our chef works with local fisherman to pick the freshest the sea has to offer for our daily seafood special. Our Roast Cod Caponata with Roasted Potatoes is an old favorite with our regulars.

Our Chicken


The perfection between the crunch of breading and the soft tenderness of the meat puts our chicken above all else!

Our Fries


Always get crunchy fries made fresh with every order.

Our Salads


Tabouleh is a Levantine salad made with finely chopped parsley, mint leaves, tomatoes, onions, bulgur wheat, and drizzled in olive oil and lemon juice.
